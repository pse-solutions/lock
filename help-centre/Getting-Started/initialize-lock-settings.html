Getting Started - Initializing your Lock Settings

1. Lock Firmware

Must be next to the hardware with Bluetooth enabled on your mobile device

TTLock iOS or Android App only

Steps to update lock firmware

  1. Select the lock you want to update firmware on, then tap "Settings".
  1. Tap on "Firmware Update", then tap on "Check for UPDATE".
  1. If an update is available, the new firmware version will be displayed in blue. Tap "Update", and wait for the firmware update to load, and once complete, you will see a screen that indicates "Success".

This process will take 30-60 seconds, and you must remain in proximity of the lock. If there is an error, you can try again, and the firmware will install on the lock.

During the update, keep the application open on the lock update page.

2. How to Set Lock Clock

Various lock features are dependent on the lock time being accurate. It's important to ensure your lock times are calibrated, specifically during daylight savings.

If the lock is connected to a gateway, the lock time will automatically calibrate, including during daylight savings.

Steps to calibrate lock time

  1. Select the lock you want to calibrate time for, then tap "Settings".
  1. Tap on "Lock Clock", and check the time. If the lock time is incorrect, tap "Calibrate Time" and the clock will sync with your mobile device's time.

3. Remote Unlock

Steps to turn on remote unlock

  1. Determine if remote unlock is already on.

If remote unlock is already turned on, you will see the words "Remote Unlock" in grey, directly under the lock name; OR you can see the blue network icon once a lock is selected.

  1. If remote unlock is not already turned on, select the lock that you want to enable remote unlock, then tap on "Settings"
  1. Tap on "Remote Unlock", then Tap the button to "Turn On".

4. Adjusting Lock Volume

Steps to adjust lock volume

  1. Select the lock you want to view the unlock status, then tap "Settings"
  1. Tap on "Lock Sound".
  1. From here, you can either:
  1. Turn the lock sound on or off by tapping on the toggle.
  2. If the lock toggle is on, you can select the volume you want to set for the lock.
  1. Once Lock Sound volume has been adjusted, click "Save".
