TTLock Overview

TTLock is an advanced smart-lock platform that lets you issue digital keys, view activity logs, and manage every 33 Lock device from one app (iOS, Android, or web desktop).

Why you’ll love it

  • Multiple key types – PIN codes, Bluetooth eKeys, RFID cards, fingerprints, and mechanical override.
  • Anywhere control – add a gateway and unlock or change settings remotely.
  • Audit trail – see who unlocked which door and when.
  • Works offline – Bluetooth credentials still work even if the internet is down.

What you need

  1. A free TTLock account – see how to create one.
  2. The TTLock mobile app (iOS | Android).
  3. At least one 33 Lock device paired to your phone via Bluetooth.
