Backup Power

The TTLock platform will notify you when the battery life of your lock is 20% or less. It is recommended that you change your lock's batteries when you receive this notification.

All 33 Locks come enabled with an auxiliary power port, located on the outer side of the lock at the bottom of the lock body.

Steps to use backup power

  1. Ensure your power bank is charged
  2. Plug the USB cable into your power bank
  3. Plug the USB-C side of the cable into your lock and the lock should turn on
  4. Enter any valid credential, open the door and replace the batteries

If the lock does not turn on after following the steps above, a physical key override can be used to open the lock.
