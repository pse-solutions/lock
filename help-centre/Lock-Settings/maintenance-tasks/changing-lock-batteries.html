Changing Batteries

The TTLock platform will notify you when the battery life of your lock is 20% or less. It is recommended that you change your lock's batteries when you receive this notification.

Use the following guide to determine what replacement batteries you need

Lock Model Quantity and Battery Type
IntelliMortise 4 x AAA
IntelliBolt 3 x AA
IntelliBolt 2 3 x AA
IntelliLever 3 x AA
IntelliLever 2 3 x AA

Steps to change batteries

If lock batteries are completely dead and you cannot enter the room, use the back-up key or backup power to open the door, and then proceed to the following steps.

  1. Open the door and remove the battery cover from the back of the lock and replace batteries. When new batteries are inserted, the touchscreen of the lock will light up.
  2. The lock will reconnect to the closest Gateways within a few minutes.
