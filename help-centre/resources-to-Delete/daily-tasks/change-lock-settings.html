Change Lock Settings

Adjust sound, passage mode, remote unlock, and the lock clock.

Before you begin

  • Administrator rights on the lock.
  • Phone next to the lock with Bluetooth ON.

Adjust lock sound

  1. Lock → Settings → Lock Sound. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
  2. Select Off, Low, Medium, High; tap Save.

Set passage mode

Keeps the door unlocked on a schedule — handy for amenity areas. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}:contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

  1. Lock → Settings → Passage Mode → Turn On.
  2. Tap ＋, choose days + times, Save.

Free TTLock accounts support one schedule per lock.

Enable remote unlock

  1. If the switch in Settings → Remote Unlock is grey, toggle it on. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
  2. A blue icon appears under the lock name once active.

Calibrate lock clock

  1. Lock → Settings → Lock Clock → Calibrate Time. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}:contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}
  2. The lock syncs to your phone; gateways auto-sync hourly.
