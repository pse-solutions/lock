Lock Management

Add, transfer, or delete locks from your TTLock account.

Before you begin

  • TTLock app logged in as administrator.
  • Bluetooth ON and beside the lock for add / delete.

Step-by-step

  1. Add a lock – Menu ▶ + Add Lock → Door Lock; press any keypad key and pick the blue “+” lock.
  2. Transfer a lock – Settings ▶ Transfer Lock(s); choose locks, enter recipient account, confirm.
  3. Delete a lock (in-app) – Settings ▶ scroll down ▶ Delete; enter TTLock password.
  4. Delete via reset button – open battery cover, hold reset, release on prompt, enter 000#.

What could go wrong?

Cannot add lock – appears grey

The lock is bound to another account—have that account delete, transfer, or factory-reset first.

Lost access after transfer

Ask the new owner to add you as an authorised administrator.
