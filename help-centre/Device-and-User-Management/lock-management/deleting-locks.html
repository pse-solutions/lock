Deleting Locks

Steps to delete a lock - using the app

  1. Log into the app, select the lock you want to delete, and then select "Settings".
  1. Scroll to the bottom of the page and tap "Delete". You must then enter your TTLock password to delete.

Steps to delete a lock - using the reset button

  1. Remove the battery cover on the back side of the lock.
  2. Use a pen to hold down the reset button until a voice prompts to "enter the initialization code".
  3. Let go of the reset button.
  4. Enter "000#" on the front keypad.
  5. A voice will then confirm if deleting the lock was successful.
