Battery Replacement

Change batteries when TTLock alerts you at ‚â§ 20 % power.

Before you begin

  • Bring fresh alkaline batteries (see table below).
  • Door unlocked or opened with key / backup power if fully dead.

Step-by-step

  1. Open door and remove the rear battery cover.
  2. Replace batteries; touchscreen lights when power restores.
  3. Wait up to 3 min for the lock to reconnect to gateways.
