Access Gateway Settings in the TTLock Mobile App

1. Log In & Open Menu

1. Launch the TTLock app on your Android or iOS device.
2. Sign in with your registered TTLock account.
3. Tap the ☰ Menu icon in the top‐left corner.
4. Select Gateway from the menu list.

2. View Your Gateway List

After tapping Gateway, you`ll see a list of all gateways linked to your account. Each row shows:

  • Gateway Type: G2, G3, G3P, G4
  • Status: Online (green) or Offline (red)
  • Connected Locks: Number of locks currently paired

3. What to Do If No Gateways Appear

  • Ensure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi network as the gateway.
  • Restart the gateway: unplug it, wait 60 seconds, then plug it back in. Pull down to refresh in the app.
  • Confirm the gateway`s power and Ethernet (if applicable) cables are firmly connected.
