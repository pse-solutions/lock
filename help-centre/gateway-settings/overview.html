Gateway Settings: Prerequisites & Overview

Introduction to Gateways

A TTLock Gateway (e.g., G2, G3, G3P, G4) acts as a bridge between your locks and the TTLock cloud. It enables centralized management, remote access, and firmware updates for connected locks. Depending on the model, range and features may vary.

Prerequisites

  • Registered TTLock Account: You must have a TTLock account. If you do not have one, sign up at the TTLock website.
  • TTLock Mobile App: Download and install the TTLock application on your Android or iOS device:
    • Google Play Store (Android)
    • App Store (iOS)
  • TTLock Web System (Desktop): You can also access gateway settingss via your desktop browser at the TTLock Web System: https://web.ttlock.com
