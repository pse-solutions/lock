Account Setup & Registration

The Getting Started Guide highlights the sequential steps to onboard your locks onto the TTLock platform. Step 1 is Account Setup, which includes Account Registration.

Account Setup Summary:

  • Download the TTLock application on Android or iOS devices.
  • Register for a free TTLock account.
  • Sign in using your credentials.

Steps to register for a free account using the Android or iOS app

These instructions explain how to register for a free TTLock account using the Android or iOS app, which is necessary for managing your TTLock devices.

  1. Download the TTLock application on your Android or iOS device.
  2. Open the app and tap "Register" in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. You can register using either a phone number or an email address. A verification code will be sent to you via text or email, which can then be entered into the app.
  4. Confirm you've read the User Terms and Privacy Policy, and tap "Register".
  5. Once complete, you will be directed back to the log-in screen, where you can enter your registered email or phone number to log in.

You can use the same account credentials to log into the TTLock desktop version here.
